Biography

I recently graduated from Military Institute of Science and Technology, Computer Science and Engineering Department. Here I was a mentor of our Computer Club, taught programming to 100+ students.

In my undergrad thesis I worked with retinal fundus images to develop a biometric system through retinal blood vessel segmentation and feature matching technique, using "Convolutional Neural Networks" under the supervision of Dr. Md. Hasanul Kabir.

Interests

My working area focuses on Software Engineering, Problem solving, developing frontends using ReactJS, building backends with Node, Express and MongoDB.

Besides these I also have interest doing research in Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing fields.
